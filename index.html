Understanding the problem

Trust in AI tools is uneven across Quebecâ€™s residents. Citizens struggle to understand what AI can and cannot do, fear hidden biases, and lack confidence in when to seek human help.

Why users hesitate

Low transparency, conflicting information sources, and prior negative experiences erode confidence in automated decisions.

Risks of misuse

Overreliance on AI can amplify errors, while underuse increases service bottlenecks. Misinterpretation of confidence can lead to harmful decisions.

Design goals

Make AIâ€™s role explicit, expose limitations in plain language, provide controllable disclosure depth, and integrate human support options.
